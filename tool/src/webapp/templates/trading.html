    Procedures and Rules

    1. Participants are credited with an imaginary cash amount of R100 000 (one hundred thousand rands). This amount will then be used by the participant to purchase any number of shares. Should the participant not have sufficient funds to process the order then the order will be partially processed.
    2. No interest will be accrued on cash standing to the credit of the participant. Participants must utilise their cash for investment purposes.
    3. Not more than 30% of the current portfolio value (the total of shares and cash not utilised) can be held in any one stock after a purchase.
    4. Any order rejected due to the 30% ruling will be partially accommodated.
    5. All transactions will be executed at the ruling price of that day's market. If a transaction is received after the end of that day’s trading it will be executed at the next day's ruling price. All transactions will be automatically dated.
    6. Definition of 'ruling price' -

      The ruling price is the closing price made available by the stock exchange at the end of a trading day.

    7. No change in the order may be made after the transaction has been executed unless it is dealt with as a separate transaction with the resultant broker charges.
    8. Transactions will be executed on the day of receipt at that day's market ruling price.
    9. Once a transaction is concluded the participant will be levied brokerage amounting to 3% of the value of the stock purchased or sold, plus the VAT on the brokerage, and Marketable Securities Tax (MST) currently 0,25%.
    10. Broker's confirmation notes will be e-mailed to participants daily showing trades executed on the previous day as well as information on any rejected orders.
    11. A portfolio statement containing the participants’ cash balance and current share value will be available on the Web.
    12. Participants may query a trade up to 30 calendar days after the transaction has taken place. No queries will be accepted after this period.
    13. Dividend Payouts will be credited directly to your account by the broker.
